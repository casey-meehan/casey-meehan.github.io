I'm a fourth year PhD student at UCSD advised by Kamalika Chaudhuri. My research focuses on personal data privacy in machine learning. I'm especially interested in the subtle problem of privacy definitions. The notion of 'meaningful' data privacy changes from one situation to another --- how to define it in a way that is mathematically precise but easy to describe in plain words is as challenging as it is interesting.
